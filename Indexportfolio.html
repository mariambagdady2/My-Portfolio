Journalist and Digital Storyteller

Specializing in Music, Film, Culture and News

I am an Egyptian American writer and journalist, and a 2024 graduate of the University of Southern California with a degree in communication and an emphasis in law. Since graduating, I have worked with social impact organizations through The Goldhirsh Foundation and freelanced for arts and culture publications, including Rolling Stone MENA, Flaunt Magazine and Atwood Magazine.

I am a Norman Topping scholar, Edith Bruce Boas scholarship awardee and an ADC Dr. Jack G. Shaheen scholarship recipient. I am currently working towards a Master of Science in Journalism at Northwestern’s Medill School of Journalism, where I plan to focus on stories that highlight Middle Eastern, BIPOC and Indigenous communities.
